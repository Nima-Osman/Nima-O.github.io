Introduction

In the following passage, we will transform a table containing unnormalized data into its fully normalized form, reaching the Third Normal Form (3NF). This process involves systematically organizing the data to eliminate redundancy and ensure data integrity. We will demonstrate each step of normalization, starting with the First Normal Form (1NF), progressing through the Second Normal Form (2NF), and finally achieving the Third Normal Form (3NF). This step-by-step approach will illustrate the principles and methodologies essential for effective database normalization.

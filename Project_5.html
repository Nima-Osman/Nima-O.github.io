Introduction

Our HR organization has initiated one-week training sessions facilitated by three distinct vendors, each responsible for training a group of eight employees. The assumption is that, initially, all three groups shared similar work efficiencies.

The main goal is to determine the mean efficiency achieved by each vendor and discern which one exhibits statistically significant improvement in employee efficiency at the 95% confidence level. This activity is designed to demonstrate our analytical approach to evaluating the impact of training initiatives on our workforce.
